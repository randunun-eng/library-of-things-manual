# Library of Things (LOT) - Operational Manual

## 1. Introduction & Philosophy

### What is the Library of Things (LOT)?
The Library of Things (LOT) is a community-driven shared resource platform where members can borrow, use, test, and learn from high-value tools, kits, and technologies instead of purchasing them individually.

**Core Values:**
*   Trust
*   Responsibility
*   Learning-by-doing
*   Community contribution

*While this is not a rental shop or marketplace, we facilitate a service to enhance client productivity: members can purchase items they have tested in LOT and wish to own for personal use.*

### Global Inspiration
LOT follows global best practices adapted to the Sri Lankan context, inspired by initiatives like:
*   Library of Great Things (USA)
*   Share Shed Mobile (Australia)
*   Library of Things YXE (Canada)

## 3. Membership Framework
*Our membership structure is designed to be inclusive yet structured. It combines a vetted entry process with a tiered system that rewards experience and trust over mere ability to pay.*

### How to Join
1.  **Obtain a Reference Number:** New members must claim a unique code from a Founder or Founding Member (who have limited allocations).
2.  **Request & Verify:** Request to join the community and complete identity verification.
3.  **Subscribe:** Select a plan and pay the fee.
4.  **Accept Agreement:** Agree to LOT rules and trust-based access.

*Membership is personal and non-transferable (exception: approved family access for Founders).*

### Membership Levels & Fees
Items are categorized by Cost, Rarity, Risk, and Availability.

| Level | Role | Fee (Rs) | Access Overview |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **Explorer** | **2,000** | Basic LEGO kits, Common tools, Low-cost DIY items. |
| **2** | **Maker** | **3,500** | IoT starter kits, Sensors, Power tools, Hand tools. |
| **3** | **Builder** | **5,000** | Robotics kits, RC systems, Advanced electronics. |
| **4** | **Innovator** | *Points* | Drones, 3D printers, High-demand tools. |
| **5** | **Advanced Lab** | *Points* | Professional equipment, Research tools. |

*Note: New members can purchase entry up to Level 3. Levels 4 & 5 are unlocked primarily through points and trust.*

### Subscription Validity
*   **Duration:** 30 days.
*   **Inactive Policy:** Accounts inactive for 30 days are auto-cancelled; users must rejoin as new members.
*   **Usage:** Membership must be active to borrow items.

### Founding Members
*   **Selection:** Based on high-value donations (including considerable contributions for community development), expertise, and commitment.
*   **Benefits:** Lifetime membership, High starting level, No deposits, Priority access, 50% workshop discount, Recognition.
*   **Family Access:** Can participate with immediate children.
*   **Referral Rights:** 5 coupon codes to give to trusted individuals.
*   **Responsibilities:** Mentorship, non-reversible donations, and accountability for their referrals.

## 4. The Trust System & Community
*At the heart of LOT is a unique Trust Protocol. Unlike traditional commercial rentals, access here is a privilege earned through responsible behavior, active participation, and community contribution.*

### How it Works
LOT operates on a **Trust-Level System**. Access grows with behavior, not just payment.
*   **Digital Platform:** A cloud-based app handles bookings, payments, and tracking.
*   **Gamification:** Members have avatars and earn points like a game.
*   **Community:** Private Facebook group for discussions and sharing.

## 5. Operational Rules
*To maintain a high standard of availability and quality for all, we have established clear guidelines governing the circulation of items and the conduct of our members.*

### Borrowing & Returns
*   **Borrowing:** Subject to active membership and level. Security deposits/NIC retention only if required.
*   **Returns:** Must be on time and in working condition. Normal wear is acceptable.

### Agreements
All members agree to:
*   Use items responsibly.
*   Respect community values.
*   Accept the trust-based control system.

## 6. Governance, Safety & Legal
*Ensuring the longevity of the library and the safety of its members requires a robust framework for accountability, risk management, and administrative oversight.*

### Security & Insurance
*   **Deposits:** Refundable; based on risk/value (waived for high-trust members).
*   **Damage/Loss:**
    *   Honest reporting is mandatory.
    *   Outcomes: Repair costs, replacement contribution, or access restriction.
    *   Intentional misuse leads to suspension.

### Liability Disclaimer
*   LOT provides access, not ownership.
*   **Risk:** Members use items at their own risk. LOT is not liable for personal injury.

### Governance & Dissolution
*   **Decision Making:** Founders can alter operations at any time; their decision is final.
*   **If LOT Stops:**
    *   Borrowed items returned.
    *   Deposits refunded.
    *   Donated items remain community assets (transferred to non-profits/schools).
    *   Founding Members can only reclaim donations if LOT ceases entirely (accepting current wear).
